Silver Sea Media is a private SME business supporting the market information and technology to the farmers of Myanmar especially in Livestock and Aquaculture. We share knowledge and information by publications and on social media.

Our Publications

Silver Sea Media is the publisher of Silver Sea Journal (Myanmar Livestock, Fishery and Agriculture Journal). The first publication was on 7th December 2005. It is the only livestock and fishery oriented regular publication in Myanmar, published monthly with 3000 copies.

We also publish the Myanmar Livestock and Fishery Directory annually since 2007. This publication is widely used in the livestock and fishery sector for finding suppliers and local shops easily.

Social Media

The digital version of our monthly publication can be read on our Facebook Page ‚Äì Silver Sea Journal. We also share daily livestock prices and both local and international news.

The page has over 50,000 followers and serves as an essential information hub for farmers.

Achievements

Silver Sea Media also organizes seminars and forums with the Myanmar Livestock Federation and Myanmar Fishery Federation. During the Covid-19 pandemic, we successfully organized the Myanmar Poultry Conference in December 2020.

Due to our strong efforts for the development of livestock and fishery farmers, we received the FAO Award in 2008, jointly given by the Ministry of Agriculture, Livestock and Irrigation of Myanmar and FAO.
